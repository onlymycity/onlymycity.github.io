What Is Only My City

Your city has a story.
Now it has a feed.

Only My City is a location-based social network where every post, every moment, and every connection is tied to a real place — your neighborhood, your borough, your city.

Real-Time Feed

See what's happening in your city right now. Posts marked 🔴 were taken within the hour — live moments from people on the ground.

📍

Neighborhood Precision

Not just "New York" — Brooklyn. Not just "Brooklyn" — Coney Island. Only My City knows the difference, and so do you.

🏙️

City Feed Filters

Follow your neighborhood, your borough, or your whole city. The feed is yours to control — as broad or specific as you want.

Rep Your Neighborhood

Earn points. Level up. Rep your block. Only My City has a built-in reputation system that rewards showing up for your city.

City Identity

You don't just live somewhere.
You rep somewhere.

When you join Only My City, you drop a pin. That pin becomes your identity — The Bronx, Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Staten Island. The app knows who you are and where you're from.

Every post carries that identity. Every neighborhood you visit adds to your city count. Only My City is the first social network where place is the point.
